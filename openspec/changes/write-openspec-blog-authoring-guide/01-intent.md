## Audience and Outcome

- Target reader: Developers adopting a proper AI-assisted engineering flow, regardless of whether they use Codex or Claude Code.
- Desired outcome: Readers understand the OpenSpec workflow from basic commands to advanced customization and decide to try OpenSpec in their own repos.
- Reading level / depth: Beginner-friendly practical guide with intermediate-level advanced section.

## Core Claims

1. OpenSpec gives AI-assisted development a reliable structure by separating intent, planning, execution, and validation into explicit artifacts.
2. The baseline flow is simple and learnable (`new` -> artifact progression -> `apply` -> validation), while still scaling to larger changes.
3. Advanced usage (custom schemas, custom skills, and verification loops) improves consistency and reduces regressions when AI output gets complex.
